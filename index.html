About Physics Tag

Physics Tag is a thrilling multiplayer game where movement is everything! Inspired by Gorilla Tag, it puts you in a fully physics-driven world where your arms and body are your tools for climbing, swinging, and dashing across environments. Join fast-paced matches, chase or evade others, and master the physics-based gameplay to dominate the playground.

Features

Physics-Based Movement

Climb, swing, and leap using realistic physics. Your skills control your movement.

Multiplayer Tag

Compete with friends or players worldwide in fast-paced tag matches.

Dynamic Maps

Explore multiple maps with unique obstacles and vertical challenges.

Casual & Competitive

Fun for casual play, yet challenging for those who master momentum and timing.
