Introduction

Physics Tag respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you play the game.

Data Collection

Physics Tag uses PlayFab to manage player accounts, store character information, and maintain game progression. PlayFab may collect data such as your custom PlayFab ID, in-game stats, and character settings.

For multiplayer functionality, Physics Tag uses Photon servers. Photon processes real-time gameplay data such as your position, actions, and connection information to synchronize the game with other players. No sensitive personal data is collected through Photon.

Data Usage

We use your data solely to provide the game experience. This includes:

  • Managing your account and character data via PlayFab.
  • Synchronizing multiplayer gameplay through Photon.
  • Preventing cheating and ensuring fair play.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data collected by PlayFab or Photon is governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • PlayFab Privacy Policy: https://playfab.com/privacy
  • Photon Privacy Policy: https://www.photonengine.com/en-US/Company/PrivacyPolicy

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. PlayFab and Photon employ secure servers and industry-standard encryption to safeguard your information.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page, so please review it periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@physicstaggame.com.
